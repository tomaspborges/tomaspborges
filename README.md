# Hi there! 👋 I'm Tomás Paixão Borges

I'm a PhD student in Political Science at [IESP-UERJ](https://iesp.uerj.br/) and currently a visiting student at [MIT](https://mit.edu/) as a CAPES PDSE Fellow. I also work as a Data Scientist at [Quaest Pesquisa e Consultoria](https://quaest.com.br/).

My research sits at the intersection of **electoral behavior**, **distributive politics**, and **public policy evaluation** — using quantitative and computational methods to understand how inequality, elections, and social policies interact in Brazil and Latin America. I'm part of the [MAPE](https://mape.org.br/) and CERES research groups at IESP-UERJ, and I've collaborated with UNDP, UNICEF, and the ILO on applied policy research.

📊 I mostly code in R and Python — electoral data, causal models, NLP, and the occasional dashboard.

🌍 Based in Rio de Janeiro (and temporarily in Cambridge, MA).
